Let us hear from our leaders about the Program..

Application service line Hyper Automation program is a combination of lean and automation with an account-level saturation model, enabling all developers and support team as citizen automation developers to bring Standardization and Operational efficiency to our apps delivery. In FY22 we established a consistent training program to build Lean squads and automation developers within every account, GIDC-India pioneered this model with many Lean six sigma certified resources and 5000+ Ninja developers who are beginners to building automation. Hyper Automation ecosystem supports all application accounts with the standard set of Lean assets and Automation assets. The delivery team is given a choice to pick their own tool of choice to deliver HAP results. FY23 strategic approach will lead us into a next-generation automation efficiency platform that enables Hyper Automation as a service.
Global Lead - AppsSreenath, A V
DXC Platform X™ is our data-driven intelligent automation platform that enables customers to accelerate their journey to resilient, self-healing IT across their entire IT estate. The platform empowers IT teams to detect and resolve issues quickly, and automatically predict and prevent future problems before they happen. Systems achieve a state of ‘silent operations,’ putting IT operations from top of mind to out of mind, saving time and money and enabling IT to focus on what’s most important: the business. Platform X builds on our commitment of delivery excellence to our customers and is DXC’s single, unified platform to ‘automate everything.’ Using the platform, customers can avoid costly business disruptions through AI-enabled proactive detection that can prevent up to 15% of critical system outages. Further, by applying machine learning to correlate alerts, incident volume can be reduced by up to 30%, enabling IT teams to focus on the real problems.
Apps Automation LeadTushar Patwardhan
DXC Apps Hyper Automation Program University helps accounts within DXC to build the automation citizenship culture. Our HAP training modules helps in building automation development career path for the entire cohort of Application Service line resources in different stages. The journey starts from Ninja, Samurai, Sensei till Shogun. By reskilling and upskilling our resources we will be able to build digitally augmented industry specific and capability specific automation at scale. The end goal is to enhance business operations and improve customer ROI. There is no single automation strategy applicable to all accounts given the various procedures, policies and organizational implications of automation. But Apps HAP training program are easily consumable, customizable depending on the uniqueness of the customer needs.
