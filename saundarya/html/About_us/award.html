TECH TANK 2023

Problem Statement-

Manual Monitoring and assigning new high-priority tickets in ServiceNow (SNOW) is typically an important task in day-to-day business. In order to maintain an SLA, every high-priority ticket needs to assign to a team member within a certain time interval (approx. < 10 min). Missing SLA will lead to next-level ticket escalation. In some domains, dedicated resources are utilized for monitoring high-priority tickets in the dashboard. Here the major problem statement we have encountered is like, the team is not having any kind of alert System when a new ticket priority is logged in Portal (SNOW).


Solution Proposed-

For this, we have implemented an automated PIAE solution bot. This Bot will monitor the SNOW for every 5 mins and if a new high-priority ticket arises it will retrieve ticket details and it will send an alert to the team via different ways like, Post a message in the MS Teams channel, Post a message in Slack, Send an Email Alert, Post a notification in Windows Notification center. Here the monitoring time frames, Alerting channels, and alert messages (Ticket details) are customizable according to the clientâ€™s choice.
